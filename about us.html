some basic information you may know about us



Gisenyi District Hospital is located in the Western Province, Rubavu District, Gisenyi Sector few meters from Kivu Lake. It is situated in a touristic town of Gisenyi bordering DRC. The hospital has twenty blocks and attends a population of over 489,398 persons speeded over 13 Health Centers in 12 sectors of the district and serves also some areas of Rutsiro, Nyabihu Districts and Goma town/Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC).
